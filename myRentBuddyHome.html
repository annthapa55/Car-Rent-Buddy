My Rent Buddy

Welcome! "My Rent Buddy" is a platform for car rental. You can register as an Administrator or a Renter.

As an administrator, you can add a new car into the system, view the cars, Search for a car, and change the status of the car. Furthermore, you can list all rented cars, and all available cars.

As a renter, you can list all available cars in the system, list the cars that you have previously rented, list the cars that you are currently renting, search for a car, rent a car, and return the rented car.

Wish you a joyful rental experience!
